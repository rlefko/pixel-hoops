# Pixel Hoops Privacy Policy

**Effective date: June 30, 2026**

Pixel Hoops is a single player game built by Ryan Lefkowitz. Your privacy is
simple to explain: the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal
information. Everything happens on your device.

## Information we collect

None. Pixel Hoops has no accounts, no sign in, and no servers. The app does not
ask for your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal
information.

## Game data stored on your device

The game saves your progress locally on your device so you can pick up where you
left off. This includes your roster, coins, reputation, training, equipped
abilities, settings, and the current run in progress. This data is stored only in
the app's private storage on your device. It is never uploaded, synced, or sent
anywhere.

## Network use

Pixel Hoops runs fully offline. It does not connect to the internet during play,
does not send analytics or telemetry, and does not load remote content or ads.

## Third parties

The app contains no advertising networks, no analytics SDKs, and no third party
trackers. Nothing about your use of the app is shared with anyone.

## Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for players of every age. We
do not knowingly collect information from children, or from anyone else, because
we do not collect information.

## Deleting your data

All saved game data lives on your device. Deleting the app removes it. Clearing
the app's data from your device settings resets your progress.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same location
with a new effective date. Material changes will also be noted in the app's
release notes.
